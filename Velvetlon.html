Velvet Country Hotel & Resort, Lonavala

Welcome to Velvet Country Hotel & Resort, a charming retreat nestled in the picturesque surroundings of Lonavala. Our resort promises a delightful blend of tranquility and luxury for an unforgettable getaway.

Price

Experience the serenity of Velvet Country starting at ₹14,999 per night.

Location

Velvet Country Hotel & Resort is situated in Lonavala, Maharashtra, approximately 80 kilometers from Mumbai. The convenient location ensures easy accessibility by road and is a perfect escape from the bustling city life.

Accommodations

Choose from a range of comfortable accommodations, including standard rooms, deluxe rooms, and suites. Each room is tastefully designed and equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, LED TVs, and well-stocked mini-bars. Enjoy the scenic beauty from your private balcony in selected rooms.

Amenities

  • Swimming pool
  • Spa
  • Multi-cuisine restaurant
  • Bar
  • Fitness center
  • Business center
  • Kids' club
  • Free Wi-Fi

Activities

Indulge in a variety of activities to make the most of your stay:
  • Relax by the swimming pool
  • Unwind with spa treatments
  • Explore hiking trails
  • Embark on mountain biking adventures
  • Engage in birdwatching
  • Discover nearby attractions like Tiger Point and Bushi Dam

Dining

Savor a delectable culinary experience at our multi-cuisine restaurant, offering a diverse menu of Indian and international dishes. The lounge bar is perfect for enjoying a range of beverages, including signature cocktails. Room service is also available for a private dining experience.

Weddings and Special Events

Host your special occasions at Velvet Country. Our resort provides versatile event venues suitable for weddings, conferences, and other events. Tailored packages and professional services ensure a seamless and memorable event.
