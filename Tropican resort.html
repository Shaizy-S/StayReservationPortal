Tropicana Resort

Tropicana Resort is a luxury resort located in Alibaug, India. It is known for its stunning views of the Western Ghats mountains and its lush green surroundings. The resort offers a variety of amenities, including a swimming pool, spa, restaurant, and bar. It is also a popular destination for weddings and other special events.

Price

Experience luxury at Tropicana Resort starting at ₹13,000 per night.

Location

Tropicana Resort is located in Alibaug, India, which is a popular tourist location in Mumbai. The resort is easily accessible by road, rail, or air.

Accommodations

Tropicana Resort offers a variety of room types, including standard rooms, deluxe rooms, and suites. All rooms are well-appointed and feature modern amenities such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and mini-bars. Some rooms also offer private balconies with stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Dining

Tropicana Resort's restaurant serves a variety of Indian and international cuisine. The bar offers a wide selection of drinks, including cocktails, wines, and beers. The resort also offers in-room dining and poolside dining.
