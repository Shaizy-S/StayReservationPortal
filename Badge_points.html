Badge Points Program

1. Badge Points Accumulation:

  • Users will accumulate badge points as a reward for their engagement and loyalty on our hotel booking platform.

  • For each hotel booking, users will receive a fixed 500 badge points. These points will be automatically credited to their account upon successful booking completion.

  • Users can track their badge points in their account profile.

2. Discount Redemption:

  • Users who have amassed a minimum of 500 badge points are eligible for a special discount of 500 INR on their next hotel booking.

  • To redeem this discount, users must ensure that they have at least 500 badge points in their account at the time of making the new booking.
  • This discount can be applied to the total cost of the next booking, resulting in a 500 INR reduction in the booking price.

3. Discount Utilization Conditions:

  • The discount is applicable exclusively to the next booking made on our platform.

  • If a user has accumulated 500 or more badge points, they have the option to either redeem the discount or continue accumulating points for future use.

  • Users are encouraged to maintain their badge point collection at or above 500 to take full advantage of this discount benefit.

  • The discount will not have a cash equivalent and can only be applied within our platform for hotel bookings.

  • This policy aims to motivate and reward users for their loyalty and continued engagement with our hotel booking service. It provides a tangible benefit in the form.
