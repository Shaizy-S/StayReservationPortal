Zara Resort

Zara Resort is a luxury resort located in Khandala, India. It is known for its stunning views of the Western Ghats mountains and its lush green surroundings. The resort offers a variety of amenities, including a swimming pool, spa, restaurant, and bar. It is also a popular destination for weddings and other special events.

Price

Experience luxury at Zara Resort starting at ₹18,043 per night.

Location

Zara Resort is located in Khandala, India, which is a popular hill station located about 100 kilometers from Mumbai. The resort is easily accessible by road, rail, or air.

Accommodations

Zara Resort offers a variety of room types, including standard rooms, deluxe rooms, and suites. All rooms are well-appointed and feature modern amenities such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and mini-bars. Some rooms also offer private balconies with stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Dining

Zara Resort's restaurant serves a variety of Indian and international cuisine. The bar offers a wide selection of drinks, including cocktails, wines, and beers. The resort also offers in-room dining and poolside dining.

Weddings and Special Events

Zara Resort is a popular destination for weddings and other special events. The resort has a variety of venues that can be used for weddings, conferences, and other events. The resort also offers a variety of wedding packages and services.
