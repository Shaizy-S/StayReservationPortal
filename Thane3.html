ibis Thane

Our Hotel promises a delightful blend of tranquility and luxury for an unforgettable getaway.

Price

₹2168 per night.

Location

The hotel is located in Thane West, about 22 miles from Mumbai. It is easily accessible by road, rail, or air.

Accommodations

The hotel offers a variety of room types, including standard rooms, deluxe rooms, and suites. All rooms are well-appointed and feature modern amenities such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and mini-bars. Some rooms also offer private balconies with stunning views of the city.

Amenities

  • Swimming pool
  • Spa
  • Multi-cuisine restaurant
  • Bar
  • Fitness center
  • Business center
  • Kids' club
  • Free Wi-Fi

Activities

Indulge in a variety of activities to make the most of your stay: Guests can enjoy a variety of activities at the hotel, including swimming, sunbathing, spa treatments, hiking, mountain biking, and birdwatching. There are also a number of attractions located nearby, such as Suraj Water Park, Thane Creek Flamingo Sanctuary, and Viviana Mall.

Dining

The hotel's restaurant, Farm Fresh, serves a variety of global vegetarian cuisine. The bar offers a wide selection of drinks, including cocktails, wines, and beers. The hotel also offers in-room dining and poolside dining.
