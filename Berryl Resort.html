Berryl Resort

Berryl Resort is a luxury resort located in Alibaug, India. The resort offers a variety of amenities, including a swimming pool, spa, restaurant, and bar. It is also a popular destination for weddings and other special events.

Price

Experience luxury at Berryl Resort starting at ₹3,200 per night.

Location

Berryl Resort is located in Alibaug, India, which is a popular tourist location in Mumbai. The resort is easily accessible by road, rail, or air.

Accommodations

Berryl Resort offers a variety of room types, including standard rooms, and suites. All rooms are well-appointed and feature modern amenities such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and mini-bars. Some rooms also offer private balconies with stunning views of the surrounding mountains.
