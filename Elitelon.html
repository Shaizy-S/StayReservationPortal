Elite Hotel

Hotel Elite is a budget-friendly hotel located in the hill station of Khandala, Maharashtra, India. It is a good option for travelers who are looking for a comfortable and affordable stay near the many attractions of Khandala and Lonavala.
Rooms and Amenities Hotel Elite offers a variety of room types, including standard rooms, deluxe rooms, and family rooms. All rooms are air-conditioned and have basic amenities such as a TV, telephone, and tea/coffee maker. Some rooms also have balconies with views of the surrounding hills.
The hotel also has a restaurant that serves Indian and international cuisine. There is also a bar and a swimming pool.

Price

Experience luxury starting at ₹18,043 per night.

Location

Location
Hotel Elite is located in the heart of Khandala, close to many of the town's attractions, such as the Duke's Nose viewpoint, the Karla Caves, and the Bhaja Caves. It is also well-connected to Lonavala, which is just a short drive away.

Hotel Policies


Check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available for an additional charge. Children of all ages are welcome. Extra beds are available for an additional charge. Pets are not allowed.

Booking Information


To book a room at Hotel Elite, you can visit the hotel's website or contact them directly by phone or email.

Additional Information


Hotel Elite is a popular choice for budget travelers, families, and couples. The hotel has a good reputation for its cleanliness, hospitality, and value for money. The hotel is located in a convenient location, close to many of Khandala's attractions.
